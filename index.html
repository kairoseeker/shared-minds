I asked the people closest to me to respond to personal questions in a voice memo — one I would not listen to. I dyed their voice waveforms, in the colour of their names, onto a raw canvas I wrapped around myself.

the canvas

each waveform,
a different voice

Raw canvas, acrylic paint, dish soap, water, ink. 60″ × 36″. The soak-stain technique, inspired by Helen Frankenthaler, integrates the voices into each other — showing the merging of all these stories into Me.

now, digital

your spine,
built by others

Invite those who know you. They answer a question, leave their voice. Each recording becomes a living waveform — woven into the structure that holds you upright.
